History Of Java:-

⇒ Below is the proper history and versions of java year wise...

1. Year 1991 :- In 1991, java was conceived by James Gosling, Patrick Naughton, Chris Warth, Ed Frank and Mike Sheridan at Sun Microsystem. Its first name was "OAK". 2. Year 1995 :- Between 1991 and 1995, many people contributed to the design and evaluation of java. Bill Joy, Arthur van Hoff, Jonathan Payne, Frank Yellin and Tim Lindholm were main contributers.
And finally in 1995 JDK Alpha & Beta version was released.

3. Year 1996 :- In January 23 - 1996, JDK 1.0 version was released. It was first stable release of java.

4. Year 1997 :- In February 19 - 1997, JDK 1.1 version was released

5. Year 1998 :- In December 8 - 1998, JDK 1.2 version was released

6. Year 2000 :- In May 8 - 2000, JDK 1.3 version was released

7. Year 2002 :- In February 6 - 2002, JDK 1.4 version was released

8. Year 2004 :- In September 30 - 2004, JDK 1.5 version was released

9. Year 2006 :- In December 11 - 2006, Java SE 6 version was released

10. Year 2010 :- In January 27 - 2010, The acquisition of Sun Microsystem by Oracle Corporation was completed

11. Year 2011 :- In July 28 - 2011, Java SE 7 version was released

12. Year 2014 :- In March 18 - 2014, Java SE 8 version was released

13. Year 2017 :- In September 21 - 2017, Java SE 9 version was released

14. Year 2018 :- In March 20 - 2017, Java SE 10 version was released

15. Year 2018 :- In September 25 - 2018, Java SE 11 version was released

16. Year 2019 :- In March 19 - 2019, Java SE 12 version was released

17. Year 2019 :- In September 17 - 2019, Java SE 13 version was released
